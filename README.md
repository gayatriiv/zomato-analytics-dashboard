# zomato market forecast dashboard (power bi)

this project is a business analytics dashboard built using power bi and the zomato restaurant dataset. the dashboard analyzes restaurant data to identify market growth opportunities, revenue potential, cuisine demand, and competitive landscape in the food delivery market.

---

## project objective

the objective of this dashboard is to analyze restaurant market data and identify:

- cities with high market growth potential
- projected revenue opportunities across different regions
- competition vs market opportunity
- price sensitivity and demand trends
- cuisine demand forecasts
- revenue distribution across restaurant segments

---

## tools used

- power bi
- microsoft excel
- github

---

## dataset

the dataset used for this project is the **zomato restaurant dataset from kaggle**. it contains information about restaurants including:

- city and location
- cuisines offered
- price range
- ratings
- restaurant type
- average cost
- availability of online delivery

this dataset was used to analyze market trends and generate business insights.

---

## dashboard features

the power bi dashboard includes the following analytics components:

- kpi cards for projected revenue, growth potential, success score, and estimated orders
- growth potential analysis by city
- revenue forecast by city tier
- market opportunity vs competition analysis
- price sensitivity vs demand analysis
- projected cuisine demand analysis
- revenue distribution across restaurant segments

these visuals help understand potential market expansion opportunities and restaurant demand patterns.

---

## key insights

- tier 1 cities demonstrate higher projected revenue potential
- some cities show stronger growth opportunity scores
- mid-range pricing attracts higher demand compared to premium pricing
- specific cuisines show higher projected demand
- most restaurants fall within the mid-revenue category
